## Detailed description

MetVUW Plus adds a set of usability enhancements to metvuw.co.nz — a New Zealand weather forecasting service showing map-based forecasts, satellite imagery, and radar.

────────────────────────────────────────

LIGHTBOX VIEWER

Click any thumbnail to open the full-size image in a lightbox overlay. For forecast maps, the legend/scale image is displayed alongside at matching height. Close with Escape or by clicking outside the image.

────────────────────────────────────────

ANIMATED SLIDESHOW

The lightbox starts playing automatically when opened. Step through forecast time periods to see the weather evolving — rain, wind at multiple pressure levels, satellite, or radar.

────────────────────────────────────────

SCRUB BAR

A segmented progress bar spans the full width of the lightbox panel. Each segment represents one time step.

· Hover to instantly preview any frame — like scrubbing through a video
· Click to jump to a frame and pause
· Move the mouse away to resume playback

────────────────────────────────────────

KEYBOARD NAVIGATION

Arrow keys work on both the thumbnail page and inside the lightbox.

  ← / →         Step through time steps
  ↑ / ↓         Switch forecast type (rain, wind levels)
  Shift + ↑/↓   Switch region
  Enter          Open selected thumbnail in lightbox
  Esc            Close lightbox
  Space          Play / Pause
  + / −          Faster / Slower

────────────────────────────────────────

SAVED PREFERENCES

Set your preferred region, forecast type, and number of days via the popup. With auto-redirect enabled, visiting metvuw.co.nz will jump straight to your preferred view.

────────────────────────────────────────

SUPPORTED PAGES

· Forecast maps  (/forecast/forecast.php)
· Satellite imagery  (/satellite/)
· Radar  (/radar/radar.php)

────────────────────────────────────────

PERMISSIONS

· storage — saves your preferred region, forecast type, and number of days locally
· metvuw.co.nz — the extension activates only on this site

No data is collected, transmitted, or shared. See the privacy policy for details.
